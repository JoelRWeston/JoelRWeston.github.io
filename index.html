🪿 Our Story

Two Silly Geese, One Love Story

From the moment they met to their civil partnership, their journey has been filled with laughter, love, and a few well-placed croissants.

🍷
November 2023

The First Honk

A wonderful walk in Victoria Park, a couple of galettes, and several wines. The beautiful journey started here with a pub crawl in what is now our beloved neighbourhood. The Chesham Arms, The Pembury Tavern, and the Clapton Hart. Marcel sat on Joel's lap and the rest is history!

🥖
December 2023

The Official Launch

Joel made the journey to Dieppe to meet Fanny and Marcel. They spent a couple of days in Dieppe, Northern France before an epic ferry journey back to England and on to London. It was here that the official instagram story launched the relationship out into the world!

🏠
September 2025

The Geese Nest!

After some incredible times and beautiful memories, the silly geese moved into their new home and started building their nest as a pair. It was a cozy place filled with love, laughter and way too many croissants!

💍
February 2026

The Proposal

Joel travelled to Toulouse with a note for his (hopefully) future French in-laws. After preparing a meal for his Belle Maman Suzette and Beau Papa Jacques, he asked for their permission to marry their incredible daughter and proposed in their kitchen. The meal was not too bad either!!

🎉
June 2026

Now Over to You!

We are so excited to have you join us for our special little party! Take as many photos as you like, celebrate love, each other, and the beginning of a new chapter in our beautiful journey together. Thank you so much for being part of our flock! We love you so much!
